Synead

I was approached by New York based musician, actor, and performance artist, Synead, to help her revamp her brand, and establish an umbrella brand that encompassed her entire body of work. I created a new circular logo/icon for her and designed a line of three shirts using the new logo and centered around the mantra, “Sync or Swim.”
