JUST GET WET.

FIDM Winter 2022 Applied Branding —— Brand Development Styleguide

Nike Swim is the newest addition to the Nike family, introducing exciting new products and accessories to get you out and onto the water.

This brand development explores the product lines for multiple water sports, including water polo, stand up paddleboarding, surfing, free diving, and more. Nike Swim takes full advantage of an untapped market, currently under-explored in their current company management.

Please enjoy the styleguide and branding instructions for Nike Swim, and I hope it excites you, as much as me, to get out and get wet.
