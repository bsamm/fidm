Golden Terra Co.

Golden Terra Co. is a small business that does custom tie-dye, screen-printing, and self-care products. They approached me as they were beginning to expand their product offerings. They were looking for a new logo that would work across new labels, signage, and online storefront branding. As a creative business, they were mostly focused on getting a base shape built, which they could then take and use or modify as needed.
