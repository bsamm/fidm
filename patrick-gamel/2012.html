2012 Bid Adieu

I was asked to design both a shirt and a social media campaign for the band 2012 Bid Adieu to announce their return to performing live in 2021. The band wanted to evoke the nostalgic feelings surrounding the aesthetic of the internet around the turn of the millennium. With the shirt and the new branding, we wanted it to feel like a piece tech convention ephemera circa 1995.
